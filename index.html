Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22st October 2024

Hit Challenge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our app, Hit Challenge.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data from users:

  • Identifiers: Advertising ID, IP address, and a self-created ID unique to our app.
  • App Events: Events set by the developer, such as game progress and in-app actions.
  • Technical Data: Information about your device, such as the operating system, device model, and hardware information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Ad Personalization: To serve personalized ads tailored to your interests via third-party services.
  • Game Improvement: To improve the app’s performance, fix bugs, and enhance your gaming experience based on user data and interactions.
  • User Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app to optimize the game experience.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the services offered by our app. Personal data such as Advertising IDs and IP addresses are stored securely and are deleted when no longer needed for their specified purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We share certain data with third-party services to serve ads and gather analytics. These include:

  • ironSource
  • Google UMP
  • Admob
  • Unity Ads
  • Meta Ads
  • Unity Analytics

These third-party services may collect data such as your device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), usage data, and technical information about your device. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

5. User Consent and Rights

By playing Hit Challenge, you automatically consent to the collection of anonymous analytics data while using the app. This includes information such as how many times a level is played and how often a player gets stuck on a specific level. This data is collected without identifying individual users and is used solely to improve the gameplay experience.

You can stop sharing this data at any time by adjusting your settings within the app.

You also have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request the deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at alidalgame@gmail.com .
  • Right to Opt-out of Ads: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings by resetting your Advertising ID or changing your ad preferences.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child, we will take immediate steps to delete that information. Our app is not directed toward children, and any content provided is intended for users above this age.

7. Security Measures

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information that we collect from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. However, no internet or electronic transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be made available on our app’s listing on the Google Play Store and on our website. You are encouraged to review the policy regularly for any changes.
